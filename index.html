Project Synopsis

Educe is a personal insight app that focuses on developing mental acuity. The idea is to provide a platform where people can not only consume content for their learning but also track their insights. In this way, people can take notes, whether it's during a lecture, a meeting, or a class, to revisit later. Educe provides the space for these insights and allows them to be categorized for easy access at a later date. These organized insights help consumers take away something valuable from their learning experiences, ensuring that they don't leave a session without their collected insights and thoughts
